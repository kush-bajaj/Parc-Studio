Frequently Asked Questions

What is your brand all about?
▲
Since the dawn of Fast Fashion, cheaper and mass-produced clothes have flooded the market. While that has been a boon to the industry and gives the customer more choice, it only helps worsen the climate. Keeping the environmental impact in mind, here at PARC Fashion, our aim is to create a sustainable, trendy and transparent fashion brand. So that the next time you buy your clothes, you know where it's coming from and where it's going to! For more information, click here to go to our about page.
How do you achieve sustainability?
▲
PARC Fashion has adopted various measures to ensure sustainability. Our pipeline uses the most cutting edge sustainable technology for production. We also ensure that our wastage is kept to the minimum, and promote recycling around the world. We have also started "Recycle Your Style" iniative. For more information, click here to go to our about page.
How long will my order take to ship?
▲
PARC Fashion ensures your order is packed and shipped within 1-3 business days. From then, the time it takes to arrive at your doorstep will depend, and you can keep track on the tracking link sent in your mail.
Do you have a return policy?
▲
We do have a 14 day FREE return policy on our items. Start a return by contacting us within 14 days of receiving your order. We do not accept returns after 14 days or on sale items.
What are your shipping rates?
▲
We offer FREE ground shipping all over India for orders above ₹3000. Domestic shipping for orders worth less than that will cost a flat ₹500 shipping charge. Global Shipping Rates and Turbo Shipping Rates are subject to change and will be calculated on your order page.
